About Our Company

Primegate Distilleries Limited is a leading distillery specializing in the production and storage of food-grade ethanol, serving clients across West Africa. We produce ethanol from the fermentation of agricultural feedstocks such as sugarcane molasses, grains, and cassava, and refine it through advanced distillation systems to achieve exceptional purity and consistency. Our ethanol is trusted across industries including pharmaceuticals, perfumery, breweries, cosmetics, and household products, where it serves as a key component in manufacturing and formulation processes. With a firm commitment to innovation, sustainability, and operational excellence. We operate state-of-the-art distillation columns supported by efficient steam and power generation systems. This advanced infrastructure ensures energy efficiency, product quality, and compliance with global quality standards. Built for scalability, our facility is designed to meet the growing demand for ethanol across domestic and regional markets. Backed by a skilled technical team and rigorous quality assurance systems, we remain dedicated to delivering premium-grade ethanol that powers industries and supports sustainable growth throughout West Africa.

Primegate is a leading producer of industrial and refined ethanol, committed to innovation and sustainable production.
